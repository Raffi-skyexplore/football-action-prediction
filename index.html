Detection Interval
Interval between pose detections (ms). Lower = smoother but more CPU. Recommended: 500-1000ms.
3D Rotation Speed
Auto-rotation speed of the 3D character. Higher = faster spin. Recommended: 0.005-0.02.
3D Animation Speed
Oscillation speed of the pose blending animation. Higher = quicker transitions. Recommended: 0.002-0.006.
LLM Temperature
Creativity of AI coach responses. 0 = deterministic, 1 = wild. Recommended: 0.5-0.9.
LLM Max Tokens
Maximum response length from the AI coach. Recommended: 60-150.
Pose Match Threshold
Threshold for pose matching. Below this = "Needs improvement". Recommended: 0.4-0.7.
Tuning order: first adjust Detection Interval for smoothness, then Temperature for reply style, then fine-tune other params as needed.
